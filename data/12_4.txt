There was an interesting announcement in the world of business today.
The NCS Group announced today that they will begin their international news service on April 1st of this year.
The broadcasts will be in the four different languages English, Japanese, chinese, and Thai.
The group hopes to add Hindi and Urdu in the following year.
However, it will depend on survey results that will be announced in January.
NCS stated that the move reflects the growing diversity of the nation's population.
